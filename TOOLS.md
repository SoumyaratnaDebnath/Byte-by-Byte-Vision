# PhD Toolkit 

A filtered shortlist of the most useful tools for day-to-day PhD work.  

## Literature Discovery and Paper Search

| Tool | Best for | Free status | Link |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Google Scholar | Broad paper search, citation graph, alerts | Free | [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/) |
| Semantic Scholar | AI-assisted search, citation context, topic summaries | Free | [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/) |
| arXiv | Preprints in CS, math, physics, and more | Free | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/) |
| DBLP | Clean CS bibliography tracking | Free | [DBLP](https://dblp.org/) |
| Papers With Code | Papers linked to code, benchmarks, leaderboards | Free | [Papers With Code](https://paperswithcode.com/) |
| OpenAlex | Open scholarly metadata and API-based discovery | Free and open data | [OpenAlex](https://openalex.org/) |
| Connected Papers | Visual graph around a seed paper | Free tier | [Connected Papers](https://www.connectedpapers.com/) |
| Litmaps | Literature mapping and update alerts | Free tier | [Litmaps](https://www.litmaps.com/) |
